Olympian Helpers

Main Idea

Olympian Helpers is a book series written by Oz Lavenski. In the story, the two main characters, Nicholas Brown and his half-brother, Francis Gorsky, do work for the Olympian gods. This includes anything from finding a treasure to stopping a war.

Characters

Nick Brown and Francis Gorsky are the main characters (or MCs) in the story. They are the two protagonists in the story. Nick is the younger kid, but he's smarter, quicker on his feet, and also the main main character. The story is writen in his POV.

Books

There are 2 books in the series right now. A third one is in the making. Each one follows a similar plot, where the kids are having their normal life, one of the gods shows up, reports something that happened, and then tells them to fix it.

Author

Oz Lavenski, as you can guess, loves Greek Mythology. He's read every book he could get his hands on (for kids, of course). He was inspired the most by George O Connor. That's where he got the myth about Hermes and the dogs and Apollo and Asclepius.
